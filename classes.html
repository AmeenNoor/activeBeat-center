Our Classes

Our center offers a diverse range of classes throughout the week, excluding weekends. With daily classes running from 17:00 to 22:30, our fitness facility ensures a convenient schedule for individuals seeking engaging workouts and activities. From high-intensity Crossfit sessions to the tranquility of Yoga, the energetic rhythms of Salsa dance, the discipline of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, the fun of Zumba, the unique artistry of Capoeira, and the intensity of Thai Boxing, we provide a wide variety of options to cater to different interests and fitness levels. Our experienced instructors are committed to supporting and guiding you in your fitness journey, fostering a supportive and inclusive community. Join us at ActiveBeat Center to experience the transformative power of movement and reach new heights in your fitness goals.
