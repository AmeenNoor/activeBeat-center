Our Classes

Join our invigorating yoga class, held three times a week on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced yogi, our skilled instructors will guide you through a rejuvenating practice that nurtures both body and mind. For those without a membership, the cost per class is a nominal 5 euros. Discover the transformative power of yoga and embrace a healthier, more balanced you.
